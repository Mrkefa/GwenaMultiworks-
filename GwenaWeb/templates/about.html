Who We Are

Gwena Multiworks Limited is a trusted name in the construction and engineering industry, committed to delivering quality, innovation, and excellence. Established with a vision to transform infrastructure and uplift communities, we have grown into a reliable partner for projects of all scales.

Our Expertise

  • Concrete Production: High-quality concrete for buildings, fences, bridges, and roads.
  • Construction: Full project execution, repairs, renovations, and finishing works.
  • Water Drilling: Borehole drilling, solarized water pumps, and irrigation systems.
  • Road Works: Road repairs, bridge construction, grading, and speed bump installations.
Why Choose Use

With a skilled team, modern equipment, and a dedication to sustainability, we ensure every project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.
