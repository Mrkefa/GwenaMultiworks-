Concrete Production

Our concrete production services ensure high-quality materials for various construction needs. We specialize in:

  • Fence concrete for durable and secure boundaries.
  • Bridge concrete for sturdy and long-lasting structures
  • Building foundation concrete for stable and reliable construction.
  • Custom concrete solutions tailored to specific project requirements.

Construction

At Gwena Multiworks Limited, we handle diverse construction projects with precision and expertise, including:

  • Full-scale construction projects for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.
  • Building repairs and renovations to restore or upgrade existing structures.
  • Roofing and finishing for a complete and polished appearance
  • Interior and exterior design services to meet functional and aesthetic needs.

Water Drilling

We provide comprehensive water drilling services to ensure sustainable water access, including:

  • Borehole drilling for reliable groundwater access
  • Solarization of water pumps for eco-friendly and cost-effective solutions.
  • Installation of water tanks for storage and efficient distribution.
  • Irrigation systems to support agricultural activities.
  • Maintenance and rehabilitation of existing water supply systems.

Road Projects

Our road projects focus on building and maintaining safe, efficient transport networks. Services include:

  • Grading and paving for new roads or upgrading existing ones.
  • Construction of bridges to enhance connectivity
  • Installation of speed bumps to improve road safety
